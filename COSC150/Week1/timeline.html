First Prototype

This module is expected to be from one of the three units in the second semester of 7th grade. Our preliminary choice is Against the Odds: Probability.

Workshop I

This workshop is intended to include the first rollout of the prototype module as well as training for the previously developed module on fractals. We will demonstrate the software and show the teachers the supporting materials created so far. We will solicit their input and make several on-the-spot changes, where possible, during the course of the workshop. The end-of-workshop version will be given to the participating teachers to use in their classrooms during their delivery of the corresponding unit later in the semester, while major changes and feature requests are worked on at Shodor. Communication should be free flowing during this time, with teachers seeing new features as soon as they are developed and providing feedback.

First Classroom Test

Teachers will use either the end of workshop version or latest development version, depending on teacher comfort level, in their classes when they teach the unit for which the module is designed. They will provide the developers with logs and feedback on classroom experience. In addition, developers will be available to the classes during the testing period as a student/teacher resource. This interaction will be recorded as much as possible and evaluated as to its value/necessity.

Revision, Iteration of First Module

This will begin as soon as the first idea, feature request or suggestion for change comes from a teacher (presumably during the first workshop, but possibly sooner), and continue for the duration of the project.

Prototyping of 2nd and 3rd Modules

These modules will be one each from the first three units of the 7th and 8th grade IM text. Our preliminary choices are Data Sense: Statistics and Data Analysis from the 7th grade and On The Move: Graphing and Functions from the 8th grade. This prototyping process will include the teachers that tested the first materials.

Workshop/Visit II

This workshop will deliver prototypes of the 2nd and 3rd modules, start the process of modification and revision on these modules, and also serve as a time for discussion of the classroom tests of the first modules. Input collected here will serve as the main guide for the intense finishing activities in the summer.

Revision, Iteration of Modules 2 and 3

Beginning during the second workshop and continuing throughout the project, we will revise and improve the 2nd and 3rd modules.

Final Workshop

This workshop will include the delivery of the almost-final versions of the courseware, and provide training in the use of the courseware for all four modules.

Classroom Test II

Teachers will use modules 2 and 3 in classes when the corresponding units are taught.

Shrinkwrap/Publication

At this point we will put the final versions of all modules into shrinkwrap form for distribution through the publishing agents.
