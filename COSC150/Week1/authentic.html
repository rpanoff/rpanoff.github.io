Shodor Philosophy of Technology in Education

  • "Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." --Plotinus/Yeats

  • AUTHENTIC: Appropriate Use of Technology Hurts! Educators Need To Innovate Constantly!

  • Computation and communication technologies ought to be used, in so far as possible, in the same ways by learners and leaders as by professionals exploring the same phenomena.

  • Technology will never replace teachers, but teachers who use technology in effective ways will replace those who don't.

  • Technology is like a steamroller: you can either drive or become part of the road.
